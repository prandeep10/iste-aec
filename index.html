The Indian Society for Technical Education is a national, professional, non-profit Society registered under the Indian Societies Registration Act of 1860. First started in 1941 as the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI), it was converted into "Indian Society foTechnical Education" in 1968 with a view to enlarge its activities to advance the cause of Technology education. Being the only national organisation of educators in the field of Engineering and Technology, ISTE effectively contributes in various missions of the Union Government. The strength of 1STE it the strong base it has in technical education institutions in the country.
